# Taco Seasoning
### About This Recipe

This homemade taco seasoning is a bold, smoky blend of classic spices designed to add depth and warmth to ground beef, chicken, or plant-based proteins. Made entirely from pantry staples, it delivers rich taco flavor without preservatives, fillers, or excess sodium found in many store-bought packets.

```
Prep time: 5 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Serving Size: 2 Tbsp
Makes 1 servings
```

## Ingredients

- [1 Tbsp] Chili Powder
- [2 tsp] Cumin
- [1 tsp] Sea salt
- [½ tsp] Black pepper
- [½ tsp] Oregano
- [½ tsp] Smoked paprika
- [¼ tsp] Onion powder
- [¼ tsp] garlic powder
